When I reached at Airport in Birmingham, I was so excited to see my college. My host advisor Faith Dobbs came to receive us and I was really happy talking to her because she is so polite and friendly in nature. Coming to the USA and studying at Gadsden State Community College is the most wonderful experience I have ever made in my life. From the very first day I felt it was an awesomely friendly environment. The staff, teachers and the students are so friendly that I never feel homesickness. I make friends from all over the world and learn about their culture. I love the facilities provided by GSCC like Library, Social activities, Food of cafe, Campus life, Club Opportunities and etc. It was great to get the opportunity to work with international club where I met with more than 23 different countries and felt amazing knowing their cultures. I went Oxford civic center where I served as a group leader and was leading 8th grade students. It was also a new experience and saw different career opportunities. I also went to Father’s heart food distribution for volunteer service where I was distributing and packing boxes and delivering food to poor people. It really makes me feel proud and was thinking at that time would that I could do anything for my country. I have joined Circle K club which helped me through various service projects. Gadsden is a beautiful city. There is so much to do here, both socially and academically. Life at the Gadsden State community college is not only about academics, I have also been able to take part in numerous extra-curricular activities and societies which I would never have had the opportunity to pursue in back home. Finally, I would say Gadsden is a place where you can learn a lot, while having fun at the same time. I am proud to be an international scholar at GSCC and I thank the college and Irex for offering their support.
